Deviled Eggs

Description

Deviled eggs are a tangy, yummy treat sure to disappear at most any pot luck event.

Ingredients

  • Hard-boiled eggs
  • Mayo or Miracle Whip
  • Mustard
  • Paprika

Instructions

  1. Cut eggs in half lengthwise and scoop yolks into a mixing bowl.
  2. Mash egg yolks with a fork.
  3. Mix in Mayo and Mustard to desired consistency and taste.
  4. Scoop mixture into hollowed-out egg white halves.
  5. Sprinkle with paprika.
