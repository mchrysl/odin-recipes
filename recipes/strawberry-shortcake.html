Strawberry Shortcake

Description

Strawberry Shortcake is a nice, light dessert that is especially appreciated in the summer.

Ingredients

  • Cleaned and cut strawberries
  • Sugar to sprinkle on strawberries
  • Shortcake cups or biscuits
  • Whipped cream or milk for topping

Instructions

  1. Place cleaned and washed strawberries in a mixing or serving bowl.
  2. Sprinkle sugar over strawberries (to taste) and mix.
  3. Place shortcake in bowl (for milk) or on a plate (for whipped cream>.
  4. Pile strawberries on top of the shortcake.
  5. Top with whipped cream or milk and enjoy!
