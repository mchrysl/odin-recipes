Meatloaf

Description

Meatloaf is a beefy, flavorful comfort food that goes well with macaroni and cheese or mashed potatoes.

Ingredients

  • One pound of beef
  • 1/2 c. diced onions
  • 1/4 c. diced green peppers
  • one whole egg
  • 1/2 c. saltine crackers crushed
  • Ketchup or tomato sauce

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F
  2. Place beef, onions, green peppers, egg, and crackers into a large mixing bowl.
  3. With clean hands (or maybe a metal potato masher> mix ingredients thoroughly.
  4. Place meat mixture in a greased bread pan.
  5. Bake for 40 minutes or until temperature reads 160°F.
  6. Top with tomato/ketchup mixture and bake another 10 minutes.
