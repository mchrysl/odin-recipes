Salsa

Description

Salsa is a great snack for the big game or any time as well as a colorful and flavorful topping for chicken and the like.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Onion
  • Green Pepper
  • Hot Pepper of your choice (optional)/li>
  • Lime
  • Cilantro (optional)

Instructions

  1. Seed and chop up tomatoes, onions, peppers, and cilantro
  2. Place chopped ingredients in a bowl, proportions to your taste.
  3. Squeeze the lime juice over the chopped ingredients and mix thoroughly.
  4. Place in refrigerator for a couple hours for flavors to blend and enjoy.
