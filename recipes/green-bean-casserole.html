Green Bean Casserole

Description

Green Bean casserole adds a creamy sauce and the crunch of fried onions to green beans for a classic casserole.

Ingredients

  • Two 14oz cans of french cut green beans
  • One can of cream of mushroom soup
  • Fried onions

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400° farenheit.
  2. Drain the green beans and place contents into a casserole dish.
  3. Add cream of mushroom soup to green beans and mix to distribute evenly.
  4. Cover dish with foil and bake for 30 minutes or until bubbling.
  5. Remove foil and sprinkle top with fried onions.
  6. Bake for an additional 5-10 minutes or until desired crispiness of onions.
